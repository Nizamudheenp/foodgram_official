Food Story

Street food is more than just a quick bite — it’s culture, tradition, and creativity served on every corner of the world. From bustling markets to hidden alleyways, every city has its own flavors waiting to be discovered. From fragrant biryanis and crispy vadas to sizzling tacos, savory dumplings, spicy curries, and sweet treats, street food offers a true feast for the senses. Each dish tells a story, shaped by local ingredients, history, and passion. Whether you’re a food lover chasing authentic flavors or a traveler seeking unforgettable experiences, the streets always have something special to offer. Discover hidden gems, explore iconic tastes, and experience the world — one bite at a time.

Where Locals Eat

Ibrayikka's Tattu Kada

A gem among food spots in Wayanad. This small home-style setup is legendary for its Dosa-Chattini, Chilli Green Chammandhi, and Pepper Lemon Pickle. The star attraction is the delicious fried Sardines - a taste that brings people back forever for that perfect Dosa-Chattini-Sardine combination.

Skuvetten Chayakkada

A hidden gem in the heart of Wayanad, offering a variety of traditional Kerala breakfast items. Their Puttu and Kadala curry are highly recommended for an authentic local experience.

Kl-12 Thattukada

The go-to place for night owls and street food lovers. Serving spicy beef fry and soft Porottas until late at night, it's a staple of the local street food scene.

Black Wolf

A modern take on traditional flavors. Known for its unique fusion dishes that blend regional Kerala spices with international street food concepts.

Dwaraka Spot

Nestled in the lush greenery of Dwaraka, this spot is famous for its homestyle meals served on banana leaves. A must-visit for those seeking the true taste of Wayanad.
